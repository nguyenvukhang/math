---
title: "Definition 1.1.2 (Complex partial derivatives)"
---

$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial z} :=
\frac12\left(
\frac{\partial}{\partial x}-i\frac{\partial}{\partial y}
\right)f
$$

$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial\bar z} :=
\frac12\left(
\frac{\partial}{\partial x}+i\frac{\partial}{\partial y}
\right)f
$$
