---
title: 'Definition 2.5 (Dual linear program)'
open: true
---

Let

$$
\begin{alignat*}{3}
&\text{max}             & c\cdot x &        \\
&\text{subject to}\quad & Ax       & \leq b \\
&                       & x        & \geq 0 \\
\end{alignat*}
$$

be a Max-LP in standard form. The dual linear program to the above LP
is

$$
\begin{alignat*}{3}
&\text{min}             & b\cdot y &        \\
&\text{subject to}\quad & A^Ty     & \geq c \\
&                       & y        & \geq 0 \\
\end{alignat*}
$$
